Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner and follow the registration process. You can sign up using your email or social media accounts.

Is JobConnect free to use?

Yes! Job seekers can browse and apply to jobs completely free. Employers have both free and premium options for posting jobs.

How long does it take to hear back from employers?

Response times vary by employer, but most companies respond within 1-2 weeks. You'll receive email notifications when employers view or respond to your application.

Can I edit my profile after creating it?

Absolutely! You can update your profile, resume, and preferences at any time from your account dashboard.
